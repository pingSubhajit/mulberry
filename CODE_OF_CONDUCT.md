# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Mulberry to be a respectful and practical project space. Contributors
and maintainers are expected to communicate clearly, assume good intent, and
focus discussion on the work.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give and receive technical feedback constructively.
- Keep issue and pull request discussions relevant to the project.
- Respect privacy and do not post another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private communications or personal information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block users, or
take other reasonable action when conduct harms the project or its contributors.
