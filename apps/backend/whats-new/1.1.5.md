## Double‑tap on the canvas to send love. Long‑press for more. They’ll see it when they unlock.

Reactions are here. Double-tap the canvas to send a quick heart, or press and hold to pick a kiss, laugh, or sparkles. The next time your partner unlocks their phone, your reaction pops up right on their wallpaper, like a tiny “thinking of you” without needing to text.

- Sticker downloads should be more reliable now, and we fixed a crash some folks were hitting after updating.
- Adding stickers and text to the canvas has a simpler, smoother flow (fewer steps, less fiddly).
- Added a privacy mode: you can now toggle it in settings or long-press on the app icon to quickly show/hide doodles on your wallpaper.
- We refreshed some wording across the app (including setup guidance) to better match how the wallpaper features work.
