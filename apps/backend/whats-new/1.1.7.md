## Keep your love story close with a new home screen widget

This update adds a new **Time together** widget you can place on your home screen. It shows how long you’ve been together at a glance, and it also keeps an eye on the next big milestone so you don’t have to do the math yourself. If you haven’t set your anniversary yet, you can add it in Settings.

You’ll also notice the canvas is starting to feel more alive, when your streak levels up, Mulberry celebrates it with a little moment of joy right in the app.

- **New:** Add the **Time together** widget to your home screen for a daily glance at your relationship timeline.
- **New pick color tool:** Pick a color directly from your canvas.
- **Better editing feel:** Text and stickers are easier to select and adjust.
