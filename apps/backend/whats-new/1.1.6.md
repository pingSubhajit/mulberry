## New style for your drawings, new reactions and more

Dry brush is a cool new style for your drawings. In the future we might add more styles, but for now dry brush is the default. If you don't like it, you can definitely switch back to the old style in settings by turning dry brush off.

The biggest change is that you can have no tool selected by default by deselecting any tool. That lets you smoothly move around the canvas, reposition/scale/rotate things freely, zoom, pan etc. We hope this will give you much more freedom and precision to express yourselves on your partner's wallpaper.

- Brush size range is now increased, you will be able to use a much bigger brush than before.
- We've added two new reactions, long press on the canvas to find out what new vibes you can send.
- You will also meet Squee starting this update, he is your companion in your journey of growing together with your partner. You will see more of Squee going forward, watch out for all the cuteness he brings.
