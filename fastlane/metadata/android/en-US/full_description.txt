Mulberry is a private lock screen canvas for you and your partner.

Send small drawings, notes, hearts, doodles, or quick reminders that appear on each other’s lock screen. It is a simple way to stay close during the day without opening another chat app or posting anything publicly.

With Mulberry, your connection is intentionally private. Pair with one partner, draw something personal, and send it straight to their lock screen.

What you can do with Mulberry:
- Draw and send lock screen updates to your partner
- Receive little notes and doodles throughout the day
- Pair privately using an invite code
- Keep your shared canvas focused on just the two of you
- Disconnect whenever you need to

Mulberry is made for couples who want something more personal than a message and quieter than social media. It is for the small everyday moments: a good morning doodle, a quick “miss you,” a tiny heart, or a silly sketch that makes your partner smile.

No public feed. No likes. No follower count. Just a private space for the two of you.

Mulberry is the new way to stay connected.
